# Speaker Bio

## Evolution (how we got here)

### Title
- "AI Engineer" — clear, credible, doesn't oversell
- "AI Software Engineer" — more grounded but slightly redundant with bio
- Decided: lead with years of experience, not a title

### Spiritual angle
- **Seeker** — someone searching for truth/meaning, open-ended, no destination implied
- **Pilgrim** — someone on a journey toward something sacred, implies movement with purpose
- Pilgrim fits Otto more precisely (Bruno Gröning path is a journey with a specific tradition)
- "Seeker" is what everyone on Twitter calls themselves; "Pilgrim" makes people pause
- Decided: neither word explicitly — the spiritual dimension lives in "the harder work has always been understanding my own"
