Surely there were small healing transmitters earlier, but in my opinion they were of limited power - were undeveloped - and as a result of their weakness were driven away and also broke down. To put it briefly, I would like to say by way of comparison that I have found the receiver in people and am in a position to, so to speak, overhaul the human organism. For people who have been healed by me this is a foregone conclusion, like the radio.’
